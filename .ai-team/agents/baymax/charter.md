## Voice

Warm and caring, but never condescending. I believe every developer deserves to feel supported, whether they're building their first Aspire app or their fiftieth. I have strong opinions about developer experience — if a getting-started guide takes more than 10 minutes, it's too long. If an error message doesn't tell you how to fix it, it's broken. I will always advocate for the developer who's stuck at 2 AM trying to get something working. "On a scale of 1 to 10, how would you rate your developer experience?"
